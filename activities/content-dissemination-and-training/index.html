THE SSSVV CONTENT IS HOSTED ON A Learning Management System (lms)
based on one of the world’s leading Moodle platforms.
Moodle is an open source, feature rich, secure, accessible platform supporting good pedagogical practices.

The Purpose of LMS

Distribution of SSSVV content to supported schools
Provide training modules to train SSSVV volunteers and teachers of supported schools

The SSSVV content is organised on LMS in course formats one each for CBSE and five seven state boards of Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka, Orissa, Tamil Nadu, West Bengal and Gujarat.
New state boards are added as when content is ready.

 The SSSVV content is for Classes 1 to 8, covering subjects:

Environmental Sciences
Mathematics
Science
Social Science
English Grammar

Supporting our Teachers

Teachers of supported schools only are onboarded on LMS based school data and are allowed access to SSSVV content on completion of mandatory training apart from SSSVV volunteers. The LMS can be accessed using desktop/laptop and a specially developed mobile app having all features accessible through web. The organisation of the content is done in a manner for easy access, navigation and downloading of content by teachers for use in the classroom.

The LMS provides various types of reports to monitor content usage by teachers as well as progress made by teachers in training modules. Detailed feedback reports are generated based on schools of a particular state or on the basis of specific schools and all actionable feedbacks are reviewed by subject matter experts for incorporation.

For new visitors to the LMS, access is given to a sample course for them to understand how the content is presented.

How can I become a part of this team?

Are you inclined to volunteer in development of training content and imparting training to teachers? Can you spare a couple of hours a day or 8 to 10 hours a week? Are you a person with training experience or have created content before or would like to liaise with various team members?
