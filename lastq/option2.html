Achievements of the Jan to March 2023 Quarter

a. Content creation and PYF mentors workshop held in Prasanthi Nilayam from 19th January to 22nd January 2022 wherein more than 150 volunteers participants. Apart from various deliberations during the workshop, an offering program was also done in the Sai Kulwant Hall on 21st January 2023 wherein each section of the Sri Sathya Sai Vidya Vahini Program were presented to Bhagawan. This website was also launched on that day.

c. Activities carried out by the Content team during the quarter:

1) Sahaveeryam -  Content Team monthly calls started out in Oct 2022 with the objectives of maintaining rapport within the group and to share best practices among different subject groups. Additionally it is a forum to share important development and information among the members of the group. Date of last call - 30.03.2023
2) Content Team Meet - This was organised in Prashanti Nilayam between 19th - 22nd of January, 2023. The main purpose was to get the team NEP 2020 compliant.
3) Ideation Module - A new approach to creating content has been worked out. The new method called 'Ideation Module' provides suggestions and optional pedagogical techniques to transact content.
4) Content Authors Training - Currently over 35 new volunteers are going through a training program on LMS. This will be followed up with online training on 'Ideation Planner' and CMS.
5) Partnering with Training Team - Content team is working with the Training team to conduct SET [Subject Expertise Training] for school teachers.
